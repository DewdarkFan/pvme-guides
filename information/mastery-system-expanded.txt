> **__Introduction__**
.tag:intro
Welcome to the expansion of our Mastery Sytem. What it means to be a master varies with each boss and acquiring a Master timer requires a certain level of strategical knowledge. Most if not all of this knowledge can be found within the respective guides; however, we have set out to define what are the most important techniques that a Master should know and use.

A Master tier player is expected to perform the strategies listed below in their submission. An Adept tier player is expected to attempt the majority of the listed strategies in their submission.

> **__Rise of the Six__**
.tag:rots
**__Master Requirement__**
<:karil:641696573272621086> <@&536406236988702750> - 46 kill hour (Incite, subject to vod review)

.
**__Tunneling__**
⬥ Knowing how to maneuver around tunnels and make efficient use of Powerburst of Acceleration <:accel:756236265472524418> potions.
    • Awareness of Surge <:surge:535533810004262912> spots and important rock locations.
        - Not dropping rocks.
    • Drink an Acceleration pot <:accel:756236265472524418> every 2nd tunnel in sync with the team.
    • Be aware of who will be making each subsequent instance

**__Follow the proper rotation__**
⬥ Proper rotations followed, which depends on what rotation it is that day
⬥ Proper use of positioning with regards to starting and running.
⬥ Manage your adrenaline, with appropriate use of Adrenaline renewal <:adrenrenewal:736298121704767538> and Combat dummies <:dummy:656844963522281473>
    • This includes reacting to occasional fast kills where you wont have time to build adrenaline back up.

**__Specials__**
⬥ Should optimally never really see specials, at most you will probably see 2 Karil specs
    • Minimum number of specs will be seen when you properly learn to two-step and follow the rotation
        - Assuming you see a spec it really depends how it is dealt with as well
    • Assuming you see a Karil spin side shouldnt be more than 21s
        - Eldritch crossbow <:ecb:615618531937222657> <:spec:537340400273195028> → Snipe <:snipe:535541258425204770> → Greater Ricochet <:grico:787904334812807238>
        - Time Snipe <:snipe:535541258425204770> to hit as soon as Karil becomes susceptible after spinning.
    • If you see an Ahrim spin that is a very bad sign.
    • Assuming bomb should still be sub 20, depending on how the kill is going.
        - Hopping sides is an important factor to keep in mind, specially if its near the end of the kill and you are East.
    • Reacting to Karil spec on the opposite side, watching out for lightning, not getting hit by bombs and attack Karil on ledge.

.
**__Mage__**
⬥ **__Phase 1__**
    • Lure correctly so that you clear tendrils and can surge to jump spot without wasting time
    • Adjusting rotation to damage/hp threshold (e.g. make use of divert/res before charging deto so that you can save your ds for P2)
    • Knowing when to Vengeance <:Veng:543478434953822208>

.
⬥ **__Phase 2__**
    • Vengeance <:Veng:543478434953822208> and Disruption shield <:disrupt:535614336207552523> appropriately.
    • Lure Telos correctly so that it’s not in the beam and your Ripper demon <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714> can attack without getting stuck.
    • Ripper demon <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714> juicing via drinking Spiritual prayers <:spiritualprayer:651079281882955787> 
    • Adjusting rotation to damage / HP threshold
        - Replacing 2nd Asphyxiate <:asphyx:535533833072672778> with Dragon breath <:dbreath:535533833391702017> is an example of this.
    • Stall ability after meeting HP threshold if the opportunity occurs

.
⬥ **__Phase 3__**
    • Get into the beam fast
    • Adjusting rotation to damage / HP threshold
        - Auto + Detonate <:deto:535533833358016512> Guthix staff <:gstaff:513203008608141314>  <:spec:537340400273195028> VS auto + Detonate <:deto:535533833358016512> Asphyxiate <:asphyx:535533833072672778>
    • Stall ability after meeting HP threshold if the opportunity occurs.

.
⬥ **__Phase 4__**
    • Don't over-stun Telos if HP threshold has been met
    • Get into the fonts as fast as possible.
    • Avoid over-stunning Telos after meeting HP threshold.
    • Avoid being overly defensive walking into 2nd font.
        - Overstunning, then using Devotion <:devo:513190158728953857 and walking to the font is an example of this.
    • Fix golem positioning if needed without losing hits on the font.
    • Heading to font 3 directly is assumed.
    • Vengeance <:Veng:543478434953822208> / Disruption shield <:disrupt:535614336207552523> before phasing into Phase 5.

.
⬥ **__Phase 5__**
    • Ring of vigor <:RoV:513201065877831680> for your Ultimates and Special Attacks.
    • Employing knowledge of autos when using defensives
        - Auto after Barricade <:cade:535541306353778689> to allow for Guthix staff <:gstaff:513203008608141314>  <:spec:537340400273195028> → Asphyxiate <:asphyx:535533833072672778> without  Limitless <:limitless:641339233638023179> is an example of this.
    • Vengeance <:Veng:543478434953822208>
    • Ripper demon <:ripperpouch:703581275453128714> juicing via drinking Spiritual prayers <:spiritualprayer:651079281882955787>
    • Take advantage of your HP, especially under Powerburst of vitality <:vitality:654618235097972737>
        - Camping Deflect melee <:DeflectMelee:544195488447201300 for  Reflect <:reflect:535541258786177064> is key.
    • Make use of beam positions and react correctly to virus
        - if it's currently side beam green and beam positions change so that red is North, then move to red beam, Entangle <:ent:567727987274022924> minions, and stay in the red beam while avoiding rocks is an example of these reactions.

    • Base should step out of melee distance if random red bomb.
⬥ Vorago should be damaged on the tick he stands after Bring Him Down.
    • Shatter <:Shatter:536256673904328704> should be on tick.
    • The boss should be vulned for this part.
⬥ Combat dummy <:dummy:656844963522281473> should be placed correctly at the end of phase according to the weekly rotation.

**__Phase 3__**
⬥ A Natural Instinct <:NaturalInstinct:513213538496675850> rotation is required.
